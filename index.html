About Me

I am currently a second year undergraduate student at the University of Pennsylvania, pursuing a Bachelors of Science in Engineering (BSE) in Computer and Information Science from the School of Applied Sciences, and a Minor in

Statistics from The Wharton School. I was born and raised in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, and was an active competitive english debater throughout highschool, having representing the national team for 5 consecutive years. My areas of interest within Computer Science are Data Analytics and Machine Learning, which are largely inspired by my passion

for debating. Analytics, in my view involves understanding given data, unravelling hidden trends within and justifying relationships if any, all while succinctly conveying important conclusions to relevant parties. In my free time, I like to read, analyse datasets on Kaggle and workout at the college fitness center.
